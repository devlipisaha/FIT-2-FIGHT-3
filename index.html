Our Mission

Why We Do This

Every year, nearly 100 firefighters die in the line of duty. Almost half are cardiac events — the majority of which are detectable and preventable through routine medical evaluation. Yet fewer than 20% of departments are fully NFPA 1582 compliant.

Fit2Fight exists to close that gap. We eliminate every barrier: no travel costs, no scheduling nightmares, no third-party labs. Our certified physicians and mobile lab come directly to your station.

  • 🏥

    NFPA 1582 Expertise

    All evaluations designed and documented to meet the full NFPA 1582 occupational medical program standard.

  • 🚐

    Mobile-First Care

    Your crew doesn't travel. We bring a fully-equipped clinical laboratory directly to your firehouse.

  • 🔒

    HIPAA Confidentiality

    All records encrypted and transmitted securely. Individual results never shared without written consent.

  • ⚕️

    Clinical Excellence

    Board-certified physicians conduct all evaluations — you get physician-grade occupational medicine, every time.
